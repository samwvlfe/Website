Creating things has always been a part of my life. I was a lego kid growing up, and that eventually led to bigger projects as my passion for engineering grew. Throughtout highschool I was in Project Lead The Way, a hands-on, project-based, STEM program. I learned all about the different engineering diciplines while building some technical experience along the way. My favorite course was Digital Electronics, which explored the world of computing and sparked my interest in creating innovative solutions through technology.
